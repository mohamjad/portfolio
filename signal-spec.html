Methodology

full signal spec (methodology)

This page documents scoring logic, reception logic, guardrails, and field definitions used to support campaign decision packs.

Versioning + Modes

  • config_version: v1 baseline.
  • modes: discovery, exploit, creator_watch, recapture.
  • markets: global defaults with market-level overrides where present.
  • logic_view_version: v1_actual_logic_linked.

Source Registry Families

  • social: TikTok, Rednote, Douyin, X.
  • ads: Meta Ads Library context source.
  • owned: TikTok Creator Centre context source.
  • reviews: official website reviews.

Core Formula Stack

score_total_expected

Weighted blend of score_velocity, score_breadth, score_novelty, score_reference_alignment, score_creator_quality, and score_confidence.

signal_strength_expected

Blend of score_total, signals_met_total, and latest_delta_total with capped delta contribution.

top_post_signal_expected

Blend of top_post_ugc_quality_percentile, top_post_accel_score, and normalized view pressure.

reception_score_computed

Weighted blend of sentiment score, inverse objections, proof demand, and purchase intent with sample and floor penalties.

Reception Config Defaults

  • sentiment_weight: 0.45 (discovery), 0.40 (exploit), 0.35 (creator_watch/recapture).
  • objections_weight: 0.20.
  • proof_demand_weight: 0.15 (discovery/exploit), 0.20 (creator_watch/recapture).
  • purchase_intent_weight: 0.20 (discovery), 0.25 (other modes).
  • min_comment_sample: 30 (discovery/exploit), 20 (creator_watch/recapture).
  • min_review_sample: 10 (discovery/exploit), 8 (creator_watch/recapture).
  • sentiment_floor: 0.45.
  • reception_watch_threshold: 55-57 depending mode.
  • reception_strong_threshold: 66-70 depending mode.

Guard Definitions

  • Weight sum check enforces score weights sum to 1.0.
  • Ratio bounds checks enforce 0 to 1 for positive, negative, neutral, objections, proof demand, and purchase intent ratios.
  • Sample penalties apply when both comment and review coverage are below minimums.
  • Sentiment floor penalty applies when sentiment score drops below configured floor.
  • Reception band mapping: weak_reception, watch_reception, strong_reception.
