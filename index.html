Open preview of formulas, configs, and guards

Core score stack

  • Weighted score components: velocity, breadth, novelty, reference alignment, creator quality, confidence.
  • Signal strength combines score_total, gates met, and recent delta movement.
  • Top-post score combines UGC quality percentile, acceleration score, and view-pressure normalization.

Reception stack

  • Modes: discovery, exploit, creator_watch, recapture.
  • Weights: sentiment, objections inverse, proof demand, purchase intent.
  • Threshold bands: weak_reception, watch_reception, strong_reception.
  • Guardrails include minimum comment sample and review sample checks.

Operational fields (sample)
