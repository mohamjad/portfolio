Saturation Map

Saturation is treated as an operational gate. We map trajectory, engagement decay, caption fatigue, and client-side conversion drift together.

Expansion Window

trajectory: accelerating and delta >= +2.0

Scale test budget up to +20% with one fresh variant per cycle.

Stabilizing Window

trajectory: stabilizing or delta between -1.0 and +2.0

Hold spend, refresh caption + first 2s proof order.

Saturation Watch

delta under -1.0 OR share-rate below 0.12% in two snapshots

Cut 30-50% and rotate narrative immediately.

Hard Saturation

declining trajectory + engagement quality under 0.01

DNS until new hook/caption stack clears minimum gates.

Recipe Gate failure treeinteractive

Watch-time dip branch
Signal: 3s hold proxy or avg watch-time drops >= 15% (client-side).
Cause: proof reveal is late or label clutter delays payoff.
Action: move final-lip frame to 0-1.2s, simplify captions to payoff + proof only.
Reception drag branch
Signal: reception under 5.0 or objection-heavy comments.
Cause: claims read promotional, proof perceived as weak.
Action: add direct objection-response beat before CTA and remove claim inflation.
Conversion drag branch
Signal: CPA up >= 20% with stable CPM (client-side).
Cause: curiosity clicks without purchase clarity.
Action: tighten caption to usage context + expected outcome, then retest.
