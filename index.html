Allocation Memo

LAKA Fruity Glam Tint (US paid launch, TikTok-first)

Objective: speed + save rate (CPA guardrail) | Constraints: first-5-second proof, low ad-feel, no clinical overclaims, ~30s max | Spend band: mid (15 concepts / 21 days)

Executive Decision

  • Fund (Greenlight 3)Lip Combo Recipe, Texture Proof Loop, Undertone Shade Finder
  • Kill (Do-Not-Shoot)Luxury empties flex, polished KR-ad pacing, payoff-late GRWM opens, launch monologues, multi-CTA framing
  • Biggest riskAd-feel / trust collapse before proof, then hold drops and CPA spikes
Greenlight 1: Lip Combo Recipe Gate (Confidence 9.6) - repeatable recipe framing, final lips in 0-2s.
  • Why now: US audience is buying repeatable recipe, not new tint positioning.
  • Hook: final lips close-up + combo labels in 0-2s.
  • Proof order: final result -> texture swipe -> step sequence -> tactile beat -> single CTA.
  • Constraints: readable labels, no viral language, max ~30s.
  • Variants: everyday nude, cherry stain, office-safe, two-tone gradient.
  • Creator lane: @adelaide_isabella, @jadenfamilo, @gabbymccarthyyy.
Greenlight 2: Texture Proof Loop (Confidence 9.1) - objection handling first, tactile proof in 0-3s.
  • Why now: sticky/dry objection is dominant and must be resolved before explanation.
  • Hook: tactile proof in 0-3s (tap, tissue press, or sip test).
  • Proof order: proof moment -> one-line comfort claim -> half-lip apply -> second proof -> CTA.
  • Constraints: no clinical claims, one CTA max, hands/lips framing for low ad-feel.
  • Variants: tissue press, hair test, cup test, +10 minute check-in.
  • Creator lane: @aolanymorie, @glambyaddie.
Greenlight 3: Undertone Shade Finder (Confidence 8.6) - resolve shade mismatch with undertone-first labeling.
  • Why now: shade mismatch anxiety blocks purchase more than color novelty.
  • Hook: undertone + shade label on screen in 0-2s alongside final lips.
  • Proof order: final lip + undertone -> swatch vs comparator -> apply -> daylight check -> CTA.
  • Constraints: show 2 shades max, neutral daylight, avoid perfect-for-you copy.
  • Variants: neutral-warm, cool, deeper-lip-base, two shades look identical online.
  • Creator lane: @carlyrivlin, @sabrinahobeauty.

Do-Not-Shoot Index (5)

  • ALuxury empties flex -> status content with weak decision proof. Replace: Recipe Gate.
  • BPolished KR-ad pacing -> US paid reads as ad. Replace: UGC pacing + hands/lips framing.
  • CExplanation-first tutorials -> proof lands too late. Replace: result/proof first.
  • DLaunch monologues -> immediate ad-feel detection. Replace: my current combo framing.
  • EMulti-CTA framing -> hostility and ad fatigue. Replace: one CTA max.

Constraints (Non-Negotiables)

  • In first 2 seconds show one: final lips, tactile proof, or undertone + shade label.
  • Sequence must stay result -> proof -> explanation.
  • Maintain low ad-feel with UGC pacing and natural framing.
  • Avoid clinical overclaims.

Failure Tree (Monitor)

  • CTR lowProof too late / intro too slow -> move proof to 0-2s, cut to 18-25s, remove brand intro.
  • CTR ok, CPA badShade curiosity without intent -> add shade certainty module and comparator.
  • Sponsored comments risePolish or over-CTA detected -> move to reply-to-comment framing, keep proof first.
