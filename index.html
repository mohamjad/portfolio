Phase 1

Signal Ingestion

We run continuous ingestion across TikTok, Douyin, Rednote, Reddit, X, and Meta Ads Library. The system looks for algorithmic anomalies: content clusters getting disproportionate distribution from engagement signatures, retention curves, and keyword behavior. Those signals appear 1 to 2 weeks before they show up in tools, dashboards, or feeds.

Phase 2

Viability Gate

Each concept in your pipeline is scored against current algorithmic state, not historical benchmarks. Proof timing, saturation risk, transfer validity, and format decay are checked against live signal output. Concepts that fail get cut with a reason and a replacement. Concepts that pass get funded.

Phase 3

Allocation Gate

Output is one gate: three concepts to fund, five to kill, creator constraints, and recovery moves if performance drops.

Phase 4

Funding Policy

Each decision maps to spend rules: pilot amount, hold conditions, and scale triggers. No taste-based approvals.

Phase 5

Governance Report

Month-end report shows what was blocked, what was funded, and what spend was prevented. This is the renewal artifact.
