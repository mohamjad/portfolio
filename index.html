How The System Thinks

The system asks whether a format is alive enough to deserve capital.

Setta doesn't ask whether something is trending. It asks whether the reception is strong or just loud, whether the format is replicating across creators or trapped in one, and whether the window is still open.

  • Comment reception quality: proof-intent, objection-loaded, identity, imitation, confusion, skepticism
  • Transfer evidence: creator-specific vs category-wide vs market-local vs transferable mechanic
  • Proof timing: whether first proof arrived early enough to matter under paid pressure
  • Saturation depth: how many creators and clusters have already run the format
  • Historical backtesting: benchmarked against comparable rows with matched objection density and transfer shape
  • Decay state: whether the format is accelerating, plateauing, or already past the window
