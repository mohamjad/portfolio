How I approach systems

Most failures don't happen all at once. They creep in quietly through missing signal, brittle assumptions, and systems that look fine on the surface.

I focus on restoring signal first. Once you can see what's actually happening, preventing or fixing failures becomes straightforward.

Early Failure Detection

Systems designed to surface issues early, before they compound. These focus on signal quality, monitoring, and evaluation so teams see what's breaking while there's still time to act.

Sentiment Analysis
