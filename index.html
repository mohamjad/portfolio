Executive Decision

  • Fund (Greenlight 3)Lip Combo Recipe Gate, Texture Proof Loop, Undertone Shade Finder.
  • Kill (Do-Not-Shoot)Luxury empties flex, polished KR-ad pacing, payoff-late GRWM opens, launch monologues, multi-CTA framing.
  • Biggest riskAd-feel and trust collapse before proof, followed by hold-time decay and CPA inflation.

Greenlight 3 (Ranked)

1) Lip Combo Recipe Gate (Confidence 9.6)

Confidence Signal 96%
  • Why now: US behavior rewards repeatable recipe framing over new-tint framing.
  • Hook: final lips close-up + combo labels in 0-2 seconds.
  • Sequence: final result -> texture swipe -> step sequence -> tactile beat -> single CTA.
  • Variants: everyday nude, cherry stain, office-safe, two-tone gradient.
  • Lane: @adelaide_isabella, @jadenfamilo, @gabbymccarthyyy.

2) Texture Proof Loop (Confidence 9.1)

Confidence Signal 91%
  • Why now: sticky/dry objection has to be neutralized before explanation.
  • Hook: tactile proof in 0-3 seconds (tap, tissue press, or sip).
  • Sequence: proof moment -> comfort claim -> half-lip apply -> second proof -> CTA.
  • Variants: tissue press, hair test, cup test, +10 minute check-in.
  • Lane: @aolanymorie, @glambyaddie.

3) Undertone Shade Finder (Confidence 8.6)

Confidence Signal 86%
  • Why now: shade-mismatch anxiety blocks checkout more than color novelty.
  • Hook: undertone + shade label in 0-2 seconds with final-lip frame.
  • Sequence: final lip + undertone -> comparator swatch -> apply -> daylight check -> CTA.
  • Variants: neutral-warm, cool, deeper-lip-base, look-same-online contrast.
  • Lane: @carlyrivlin, @sabrinahobeauty.

Do-Not-Shoot Index (5)

  • Luxury empties flexStatus content with low decision proof. Replace with Recipe Gate.
  • Polished KR-ad pacingTriggers ad-feel in US paid. Keep sequencing, switch to UGC pacing and hands/lips framing.
  • Explanation-first tutorialsProof lands late and hold decays. Start with result or proof.
  • Launch/brand monologuesImmediate auction-level ad detection. Reframe as current combo routine.
  • Multi-CTA framingRises hostility and lowers intent. Keep one CTA maximum.

Constraints (Non-Negotiables)

  • 0-2s proof gateShow one immediately: final lips, tactile proof, or undertone + shade label.
  • Story orderResult -> proof moment -> explanation. Never explanation-first.
  • Ad-feel controlUGC pacing and natural framing only; avoid polished product-hold compositions.
  • Claim safetyNo clinical overclaims or universal-performance copy.

Failure Tree (Monitor + Fix)

  • CTR lowLikely proof too late or intro too slow. Move proof to 0-2s, shorten to 18-25s, remove brand intro.
  • CTR ok, CPA badShade curiosity without intent. Add shade-certainty module and comparator clarity, tighten CTA.
  • Sponsored comments risePolish or over-CTA issue. Switch to reply-to-comment framing and restore proof-first sequence.
